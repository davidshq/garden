# Core Simplicity
## Introduction
I have an insatiable hunger
   for knowledge and experience. 
It gnaws at me
   from the inside out. 
I want to and sometimes need
   to know.

Yet here is the wisdom I've learned
   and that more at the hands of the simple
   than in the halls of the great:

   *I am not as grand as I think I am.*

   *I can live meaningfully while being small.*

   *The simple truths are the ones I need to hear, practice, remember most often.*

   *Better one thing well than a hundred poorly.*

Knowledge, experience - these are pleasures
   not necessities. 
When I make them necessities 
   I miss out on life. 
Which reminds me of one more lesson:
   *I'm terrible at following simple wisdom.*
