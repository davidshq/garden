## Living
### CBT / ERP
**Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** and **Exposure Response Prevent (ERP)** are methods and treatments for distorted cognitions. They can help heal mental disorders as well as the more run-of-the-mill day-to-day distortions we all experience.
#### Recommendations
- David Burns. [Feeling Good: The New Mood Therapy](https://amzn.to/4fQmW0l).
### EFT and Attachment Theory
**Attachment Theory** explains why we behave as we do, particularly in relationships. **Emotion Focused Therapy** offers a framework, methods, and treatments for working with the dysfunctional parts of our internal and interpersonal attachment systems.
#### Recommendations
- Sue Johnson. [Hold Me Tight: Seven Conversations for a Lifetime of Love](https://amzn.to/4fWmxJI).
- Amir Levine, Rachel S. F. Heller. [Attached: The New Science of Adult Attachment and How It Can Help You Find--And Keep--Love](https://amzn.to/4fPACse).
### Neurodivergent
Some of us are different different. Our ways of cognition and experiencing the world do not conform to the statistical norm. This is known as "neurodivergence." Learning to live in a world that primarily teaches and expects us to behave in neurotypical ways is challenging, overwhelming, heart-rending.

Learning about neurodivergence helps those of us who are neurodivergent manage our own lives and can help others in interacting productively and lovingly with the neurodivergents in their lives.
#### Recommendations
- Russell A. Barkley. [Taking Charge of Adult ADHD: Proven Strategies to Succeed at Work, at Home, and in Relationships](https://amzn.to/3HOkieH).
- Melissa Orlov. [The ADHD Effect on Marriage: Understand and Rebuild Your Relationship in Six Steps](https://amzn.to/45FkWmE).

### Codependence
**Codependence** is an unhealthy form of attachment. Unhealthy attachments can experience healing through EFT, may be intensified by neurodivergence, and can often respond to CBT/ERP treatments.

CoDA (**Codependents Anonymous**) is a twelve-step program for those struggling with codependence.
#### Recommendations
- Anne Wilson-Schaef. [Co-Dependence: Misunderstood--Mistreated](https://amzn.to/3Hq3zOY).
- Anonymous. [Co-Dependents Anonymous Big Blue Book](https://www.corepublications.org/product/coda-book).
