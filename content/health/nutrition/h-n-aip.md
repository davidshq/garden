# Forbidden
One initially avoids eating any forbidden foods. After a period of time on the strict elimination AIP one can begin to introduce foods one by one. There are four stages that one is meant to introduce - starting with Stage 1 and moving on up through Stage 4. The idea being that the items in Stage 1 are less likely to be the cause of issues than those in higher stages.
