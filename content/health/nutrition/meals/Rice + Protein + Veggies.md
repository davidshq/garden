## Rices (Carbohydrates)
Rices are a surprisingly (to me) good source of calories. 100 grams of any rice clocks in at 350 - 370 calories!

They also pack a decent amount of protein (7 - 12.8 grams). They are a good source of fiber (2.8 - 4.3 grams), phosphorus, potassium, zinc, niacin, Vitamin B-6, and biotin.
