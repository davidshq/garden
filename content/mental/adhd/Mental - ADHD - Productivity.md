#### Single Tasking
- To a single task at a time. (APB)
#### Timers
- Use a timer (20, 30, 60 mins) to focus on a specific task for a limited period of time. (APB)
#### Clutter
- Clear clutter from workspace. (APB)
	- Doesn't need to be done, just out of sight.
#### To Do List
- Keep the to-do list short. (APB)
	- See Zeigarnik Effect
	- Move items that aren't urgent onto "wish list", have task to review wish list.
