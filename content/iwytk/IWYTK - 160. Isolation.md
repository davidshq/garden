When I feel crappy I usually don't want to be around people. Sometimes there are those select few who can be neutral or even positive in their effect on me and that is a beautiful thing. But so often I find myself simply wanting to withdraw from the world and to be by myself.

Sometimes this is necessary. Sometimes we need some time away to recharge our batteries but don't let it become a persistent way of living.

Force yourself to spend time with other people.

For me, I've had periods where I really couldn't do anything (physically, psychologically) so I'd watch a movie with someone. I didn't even have to move.

We don't want to make that the way we live all the time, but if we can't do anything more, doing it with someone is usually better than alone.
