There are a number of systems that outline what "virtues" are. Here are some of them:
# Character Strengths and Virtues
- Christopher Peterson, Martin Seligman.
- Divides into 6 virtues which contains 24 strengths.
1. Wisdom and Knowledge
	1. Creativity
	2. Curiosity
	3. Judgment (critical thinking)
	4. Love of Learning
	5. Perspective
2. Courage
	1. Bravery
	2. Perseverance
	3. Honesty (integrity)
	4. Zest
3. Humanity
	1. Love
	2. Kindness
	3. Social Intelligence
4. Justice
	1. Teamwork
	2. Fairness
	3. Leadership
5. Temperance
	1. Forgiveness
	2. Humility
	3. Prudence
	4. Self-Regulation
6. Transcendence
	1. Appreciation of Beauty and Excellence
	2. Gratitude
	3. Hope
	4. Humor
	5. Spirituality

# The Book of Virtues and The Moral Compass
- William J. Bennett
- The Book of Virtues covers..
	- Self-Discipline
	- Compassion
	- Responsibility
	- Friendship
	- Work
	- Courage
	- Perseverance
	- Honesty
	- Loyalty
	- Faith
- The Moral Compass covers...
	- Home and Hearth
	- Into the World
	- Standing Fast
	- Easing the Path
	- Mothers and Fathers, Husbands and Wives
	- Citizenship and Leadership
	- What We Live By
